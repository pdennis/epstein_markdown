# AGENDA

- The economics of dispute resolution and enforcement actions from the corporate perspective
- How dispute resolution funding can support a cash preservation/asset monetization strategy
- When advisors should consider dispute resolution funding as an option for their clients
- Explanation of the process of assessing and reviewing potential claims
- The importance of an independent financial review and assessment of cash and non-cash positions
