# PROPOSED ORDER

S2 20 Cr. 330 (AJN)

ALISON J. NATHAN, United States District Judge:

WHEREAS the Court has ordered that certain witnesses will be permitted to testify either using pseudonyms or only using first names (the "Protected Witnesses"). See 11/01/21 Tr. at 11:13-17);

WHEREAS to effectuate that order, it is necessary to prohibit courtroom sketch artists from drawing exact likenesses of the Protected Witnesses;

IT HEREBY IS ORDERED:

1. That courtroom sketch artists - whether in the courtroom or overflow rooms - may not draw exact likenesses of the Protected Witnesses; and
