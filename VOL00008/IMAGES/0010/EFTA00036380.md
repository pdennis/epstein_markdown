Employee Locator Information

INSTRUCTIONS: This form is to be completed by all employees upon initial employment (excessive) and whenever any information on the form changes. Changes should be promptly recorded by submission of this form to your personnel office. In accordance with the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, submission of some data is MANDATORY, other data is requested on a VOLUNTARY basis for the safety and convenience of the employee. The MANDATORY or VOLUNTARY nature of the data is indicated in the applicable Privacy Act Statement. Certain portions of the form are to be completed by the employer's personnel office or administrative officer. Employees should leave these portions of the form blank. If you have any questions regarding completion of the form, please consult your personnel office.
