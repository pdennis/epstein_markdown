# Nova

## To Set-up the Tablet:
- Place the heated table, sitting on the rubber band, using only the level of your hand, being the job between the first and bottom and the table wall open. (Fig. 1)
- Avoid the front up until the legs are fully extended, keeping the face rest. (Fig. 2)

## To Set the Table Upright:
- When the table is fully opened, tilt the table upright until all feet has not on the floor. Let one end of the table, press down of the outside and remove the cables a little. (Fig. 3)

## To Change the Table Height:
- Choose the hole that represents the desired length and after removing the back, put the bolt through that hole. Bother and tighten the knife by hand until using. Repeat on all other leg extensions, setting all extensions at same length. (Fig. 4)

## To Clean Box Sitting:
- Turn the table on its side, with the rubber band extension. If there is a fracture, place it under the table by pressing the attached center down into the disk extended. (Fig. 5)
- Partially close the table row.
- Fold the legs into the table and make sure the armwork being cut and the cables are inside of the table. Hold the two halves of your table together to lock. (Fig. 6)

## For Tables with the Shiawa Option:
- With your table on its side, release the cables from the rings (Fig. 8a) and turn the unshock clamp to the side. (Fig. 8b)
- Fold the legs against the underside and lay the table down flat on the floor. (Fig. 9)
- To return to normal height settings, use the separate CAUTION label that refers to the Shiawa Cable Option.
